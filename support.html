Frequently Asked Questions

If we haven't answered your questions, please feel free to use the contact form above
Is Tribe free to use?

You can search for free, but you need to be on our Premium plan to download .CSV files.

Why should I download lists?

Twitter doesn't offer great targeting. Tribe allows you to download curated Twitter lists so that you can target your exact audience.

Is there a free plan?

No, but you can search Tribe for free. Our premium plan is reasonably priced and helps cover our costs.

What information do you collect in the .csv?

We collect the list link, list name, type (list subscriber/member), user handle, user's name, user's location, user's bio, user's followers + following and number of statuses.

What do I do once I've downloaded a list?

Once you've downloaded your list, you can upload the file to Twitter and it'll automatically target those users.

How do I open a .csv file?

You can use Excel or similar spreadsheet software to open your .csv file. You won't need to edit the file, but you can check the .csv quality.
